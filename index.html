Welcome to the Snake Game


The history of the game

The journey of Snake began in the late 1970s when arcades started gaining popularity. The first record of the Snake game dates back to 1976 when it appeared as a primitive version on the Blockout computer developed by Gremlin Industries. However, it was in 1977 that the game gained popularity with the release of the Apple II console, where programmer Steve Wozniak developed a simple version of the game as a showcase of the computer's graphic capabilities.

What is the Snake Game?

The game's concept is simple yet addictive. Players control a snake that grows as it consumes items, usually represented by points. The goal is to avoid colliding with the maze walls or the snake's own body. The difficulty increases as the snake grows, making it more challenging and requiring quick thinking and agile reflexes.
